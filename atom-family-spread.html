Atom Family

Illustration / Print

My father, a neurologist at Johns Hopkins University, is the center of my family dynamic. I thought the atom structure best reflected the cohesive nature of my family, as well as my father's domineering role in the Ricaurte household. In the background are the faded molecular structures of compounds such as serotonin and dopamine, the kinds of chemicals my father works with in his studies of the brain and its neurochemistry.
