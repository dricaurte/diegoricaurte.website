Tournament of Heroes

Illustration / Print

This poster was inspired by an episode of the television show "Teen Titans" entitled "Winner Take All". In this episode, Boy Wonder Robin and his super hero friends are placed in a "Tournament of Heroes", in which one teen hero will be crowned the Champion of Champions. Robin makes it to the final round and goes toe to toe with Speedy, a bow wielding hero with whome he bears an uncanny resemblance. Their rivalry, as well as the duplicity of their appearance, inspired this design
